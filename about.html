About Time Refined

Meet the Editor: Dev Shah

Welcome to Time Refined! I'm Dev Shah, the founder and chief enthusiast here. My journey into watches started unexpectedly, but the blend of micro-engineering, history, and design quickly became a passion. I realized that navigating the world of horology can be daunting, yet incredibly rewarding.

I created Time Refined to be a clear, engaging, and reliable resource for everyone interested in watches – from the curious newcomer to the dedicated collector. We focus on providing honest reviews, explaining complex topics simply, and showcasing the beauty of timepieces. Our mission is to share the fascination of horology and help you find watches you'll love and appreciate.

Our Journey (Fictional History)

From a simple blog to a dedicated watch resource – Time Refined's story.

April 2023: Initial Wind

Inspired by a burgeoning passion for horology and the lack of a single, accessible resource for newcomers, Dev Shah begins documenting his personal journey into watches. What started as informal notes and forum posts quickly evolves into a simple blog. He shares his discoveries, explains complex terms in understandable language, and reviews affordable watches, finding an unexpectedly eager audience of fellow enthusiasts looking for clear, relatable content.

November 2023: Setting the Hands

Recognizing the potential to build a more comprehensive resource, Dev decides to structure the growing collection of information. The blog is rebranded as "Time Refined." Around this time, Alex Gear, a watchmaker and technical expert, joins the team. Alex's expertise adds a layer of technical rigor to the reviews and articles, ensuring accuracy and deeper insights into movements and complications. This collaboration marks a significant step towards becoming a trusted source of information.

May 2024: Polishing the Case

As the site gains traction, the need for improved presentation and broader content becomes clear. Jamie Dial, a graphic designer with a keen eye for aesthetics, is brought on board to elevate the site's visuals, focusing on high-quality photography and a clean, user-friendly interface. Simultaneously, Casey History joins the team, contributing in-depth historical features on iconic watches and brands, curating content from online communities, and fostering greater engagement with the growing readership. The site begins to host Q&A sessions and interactive polls.

December 2024: Complicating the Movement

Time Refined expands its content offerings to include guides on watch maintenance, strap changing tutorials, and interviews with figures in the watch industry. They begin developing a searchable database of watch specifications and reviews. The team focuses on creating content that demystifies more complex aspects of horology, such as different types of finishes, the intricacies of various movements, and the history of specific complications, solidifying their position as a valuable educational resource.

2025: Keeping Time

Time Refined proudly unveils its comprehensive, redesigned website. The new platform integrates all their content – reviews, historical articles, guides, gallery, and community features – into a seamless experience. With a dedicated team and a clear vision, Time Refined is committed to being a clear, trustworthy, and engaging resource for watch enthusiasts everywhere, from those just starting their journey to seasoned collectors, helping them explore the captivating world of timekeeping.
